The Vision

I am a developer and designer focused on the intersection of deep learning and mobile user experiences. This portfolio serves as a curated archive of my technical journey—ranging from optimizing neural network architectures to crafting intuitive React Native interfaces. Beyond a course requirement, this space is a commitment to "learning in public," documenting the iterations of my projects and the evolution of my personal brand.

My brand is built on technical rigor and editorial clarity. My audience includes potential collaborators in the AI research space and creative directors in digital design. By showcasing not just the final product, but the underlying logic—be it a gradient calculation or a UI flow—I aim to demonstrate a holistic approach to modern software development.

As you navigate through this site, you will find a breakdown of my work with the VizWiz dataset, mobile applications designed for travelers, and my explorations in contemporary digital art. I believe that clean code and clean design are inseparable, and this portfolio is an extension of that philosophy. I hope you find inspiration in these entries and reach out for collaboration.
