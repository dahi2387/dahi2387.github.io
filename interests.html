Interests

Contemporary Editorial Design

I have a deep passion for the intersection of contemporary art and digital layouts. I enjoy creating digital platforms that act as artist profiles, focusing on how long-form text and imagery can coexist in a responsive mobile environment without losing the "gallery" feel of a physical publication.

Mathematical Typesetting

Beyond coding, I find a unique satisfaction in the precision of LaTeX. I utilize it for formatting complex technical documents and academic problem sets, ensuring that mathematical notation is not only accurate but visually elegant through careful management of environments and spacing.

Mobile UI/UX Exploration

I spend my free time experimenting with React Native components, specifically focusing on custom font integration and interactive map features. I love the challenge of taking a complex dataset and making it accessible through a clean, gesture-driven mobile interface that feels native to the user.
